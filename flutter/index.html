Flutter Apps

Here I have my flutter projects, neither are anything special, in fact they're rather useless and hardly of my own creation.

They should both be Progressive web app compatible, depending on your browser of course,
When in the webapp: on chrome/brave there should be a little plus icon at the right of the address bar to install it and on safari ios just tap share then add to home screen. I'm unsure about safari osx. Firefox doesn't appear to have any PWA support, not ot say it doesn't work as a webpage.

A small note although theses pages may use HTML5 canvas, I do not do any tracking of any sort, unless flutter has some sort weird of tracking built in.

Binary Clock

This is really just a carbon copy from this Fireship.io tutorial, with the only differences being that I omitted all the deprecated code. This is designed to only work in landscape, it will have issues on a portrait screen. There is no real functionality to this other than it being a clock. Looks neat though.

Cupertino App

This is also just a carbon copy of the video at the top of the Flutter For iOS Developers page, nothing interesting or functional just a web-based flutter iOS app with a few little Cupertino things. It should function just fine in both landscape and portrait. It should also have dynamic light and dark modes, although some back text stays black in dark mode.
